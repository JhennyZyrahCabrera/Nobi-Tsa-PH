About Us

Our Story

It began with a humble bubble tea shop, originally a modest single-story building that transformed into our cherished store. Adorned with a handful of chairs and tables, our customers adored it, propelling us towards becoming the revered bubble tea destination within our community. After two years of dedicated efforts, we triumphantly elevated our establishment from a small, single-story structure to a grand two-story building, ultimately blossoming into a full-fledged cafe.

"At first it is owned by my father who is a barangay captain in our community and it was originally named "nobicha" and it was inspired by the main character in anime doramon, Nobita. It was inspired by nobita because my father knows that nobita is my favorite charater when I was a child up until now, then since my father is a barangay captain and his schedule is tight, he decided to pass the management of the store to me, at first I was scared but as I see the satisfaction in my costumers eyes, I got inspired to continue the journey of our store. That time I knew it was getting famous, I decided to change its name to nobi Tsa PH and upgrade the store, the rest is history."
